# Regimen


## Getting up / The Morning Dance


### In bed
* 10 hip-ups
* 5 leg raises

### Peeing
* 10 head look up / look down
* 10 look left look right

### In shower
* 10 squats
* Touch the toes

### In front of basin

* Wiggle while brushing teeth
* Laugh and sing

### In the kitchen
* Do the dishes while dancing
* 1 to 3 minute dedicated dance



### During the day

* 5,000 steps
* Heart rate
* 18 minutes
* 3 Minuts
